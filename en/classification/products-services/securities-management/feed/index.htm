]]> Musharaka Saudi Equities Fund is an open-ended investment fund compliant with Islamic Shariah standards. The fund aims to achieve capital growth over the medium and long term by investing in shares of companies listed in financial markets and initial public offerings (IPOs) in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia, whether in the main market or the parallel market (Nomu). The fund is permitted to invest across various equity sectors listed in financial markets, as well as in money market funds, sukuk funds, real estate investment traded funds (REITs), and exchange-traded funds (ETFs). In 2016, the fund was awarded the Best Shariah-Compliant IPO Fund in the Middle East.
