Account Opening Requirements

Before starting your investment account application at Musharaka Financial, you must be registered and have an active account with one of the approved digital wallet platforms.

1

Core Requirement

All clients wishing to open an investment account at Musharaka Financial must have an active account with one of the digital wallet platforms listed below. This account is used for identity verification and financial transfer purposes in accordance with Saudi Capital Market Authority regulations.

If you do not have an account on any of the digital wallet platforms, it is recommended to create one on the platforms listed below before starting your account application to ensure a smooth process.
